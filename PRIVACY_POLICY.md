BTVois ("we" or "our" or "us") respects the privacy of its users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (BTVois).

- We collect voice input for invoice generation purposes
- All voice processing is done offline on your device
