# Privacy Policy for BTVois

Last updated: December 5, 2025

## Introduction

Chikoochi ("we" or "our" or "us") respects the privacy of its users ("user" or "you"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (BTVois).

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use Btvois, we may collect:

- **Account Information**: Email address, phone number, and display name when you sign in with Google or phone authentication
- **User Profile**: Business name, logo, and other profile details you provide
- **Authentication Data**: User ID and authentication tokens managed by Firebase Authentication

### Voice Data

- We collect voice input for invoice generation purposes
- All voice processing is done offline on your device using the device's built-in speech recognition
- We do not record, store, or transmit your voice data to our servers

### Invoice and Business Data

- Invoice details, customer information, and transaction records are stored locally on your device
- For authenticated users, invoice data is synced to Firebase Firestore for backup and cross-device access
- All data transmission uses encrypted connections (HTTPS/SSL)

### Usage Data

- We collect anonymous usage statistics through Firebase to improve app performance
- This includes crash reports, app version, and device information (OS version, model)
- No personally identifiable information is included in analytics

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Authenticate and manage your account
- Sync your invoice data across devices (if logged in)
- Provide backup and restore functionality
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Process in-app purchases and manage subscription status
- Send service-related notifications

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage**: Invoice and business data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Hive/Flutter Secure Storage)
- **Cloud Backup**: Authenticated users have their data backed up to Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit
- We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption for all network communications
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

BTVois uses the following third-party services that may collect user data:

### Firebase (Google LLC)

- **Firebase Authentication**: Manages user sign-in with Google or phone number
- **Cloud Firestore**: Stores encrypted backup of invoice data for authenticated users
- Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

### Google Sign-In

- Collects email address, name, and profile photo when you choose to sign in with Google
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Google Play In-App Billing

- Processes in-app purchases for premium features
- Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Speech Recognition

- Uses your device's built-in speech recognition (Android Speech API)
- Voice data is processed locally; we do not receive voice recordings
